# Nvidia Market Note

Nvidia continues to benefit from strong demand tied to AI infrastructure, data center spending, and accelerator supply constraints.

Potential drivers to monitor:
- AI and data center demand
- Gross margin trends
- Supply availability
- Competition in accelerators and inference hardware
- Customer concentration in large cloud buyers

Potential risks:
- Valuation sensitivity
- Demand volatility after strong cycles
- Regulatory or export restrictions
- Margin pressure if supply catches up faster than demand
- Competitive pressure from alternative chips
