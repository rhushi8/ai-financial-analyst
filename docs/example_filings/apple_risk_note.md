# Apple Risk Note

Apple is often viewed as a high-quality large cap company, but it still faces meaningful operating risks.

Potential risk areas:
- China supply chain concentration
- Hardware cycle dependence
- Regulatory pressure on app store policies
- Slowing device replacement cycles
- Currency and macro sensitivity

Potential positive offsets:
- Large installed base
- Services revenue mix
- Strong brand loyalty
- Consistent cash generation

This sample document is designed to test retrieval on risk-related queries.
